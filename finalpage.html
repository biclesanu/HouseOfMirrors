A 5 Painting set exploring the theme of self- discovery and self-change.


Author: Alisa Biclesanu
House of Mirrors
The state you find yourself in when you finally manage to see who you are "from above". You finally understand what masks you are wearing and why, so you can now see right through them. Only the first step in your journey.
This is the first mirror, the one in which you notice the source of your biggest flaws.
MIRROR #2
Stripped down of worldly worries, you are nothing but what you feel. You question everything you've learned about the world and you lay the ground for a new set of values to settle in. The illusion of a revelation... It feels serene but it's temporary. This is the mirror that teaches self compassion.
MIRROR #3
After processing so many things about your old self and learning these harsh new lessons, you expect a new life to unfold. However, the roots of your old habits are well anchored. You feel stuck, caught, as if the pain you've endured in order to get here is meaningless. From here on now, the process is slower, but the insight is worthwhile.
This is the mirror that shows you the path to befriending your flaws.
MIRROR #4
The path to discovering the self is paved with a lot of trauma processing, a painful experience in which you sit with your internalized fears and pain. This is one of the hardest parts of the journey, and the benefits are to come much later. For now, you sit with some painful realizations.
The mirror that shows us how external factors wrote our destiny.
The final mirror, completely different from the others. At times, you get a brief glimpse into this mirror, and you feel a sense of where things might be or should be headed. It shows you your true potential, or just something to aspire towards. It's a replacement for the first mirror, showing you it's time to move towards something new.
